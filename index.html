The Founder's Note

"We do not create jewellery to show wealth; we create jewellery to capture light, memory, and enduring love across generations." — Henri Aurelisse (Paris, 1912)

Every diamond selected by our gemologists must exceed strict optical criteria above and beyond standard GIA certifications. Our master artisans hand-bevel each setting pricker to a microscopic knife-edge, ensuring maximum surface area remains exposed to ambient light while guaranteeing permanent stone retention.
