The Heritage of Quiet Restraint

For well over a century, the House of Aurelisse has stood as a beacon of uncompromising Parisian high jewellery. Established in 1894 by master lapidary Henri Aurelisse, our atelier was born from a singular, rebellious philosophy: true luxury never shouts. While European courts indulged in ornamental excess, Aurelisse championed the purity of form, architectural balance, and the unadorned fire of exceptional stones.

Each masterpiece within our Place Vendôme archive is hand-forged by Senior Jewelers who have dedicated lifetimes to mastering the traditional art of micro-pavé setting, articulated gold latticework, and platinum forging. We source only GIA-certified D-to-F color diamonds of VVS and Flawless clarity, alongside untreated rare colored gemstones from the world's most responsible and historically significant mines.

Today, Aurelisse remains dedicated to this quiet nobility. We do not design seasonal decorations; we commission heirlooms destined to transcend generations. Whether experiencing our creations in our Place Vendôme salon or via our dedicated digital concierge, you are acquiring a piece of enduring French artistry.
